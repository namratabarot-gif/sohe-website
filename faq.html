FAQ

Helpful answers for first-time SOHE customers

A clear overview of how the toothpaste feels, who it suits, and what to expect with regular use.

Who is SOHE designed for?

It is a strong fit for people who want a more premium daily toothpaste with a focus on enamel support, polished brightness, and a gentler feel.

Can I use it every day?

Yes. The formula is intended for twice-daily use as part of a normal brushing routine.

Does it contain harsh bleaching agents?

No. The brightening effect comes from polishing and smoothing rather than strong bleach-led whitening.

Will it help with sensitivity?

Many customers choose it specifically because the formula is designed around mineral support and a less aggressive brushing experience.

How long does one tube last?

For one person using a small amount twice daily, a tube generally lasts around one month.

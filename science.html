Hydroxyapatite

The mineral your teeth already know

Hydroxyapatite closely resembles the mineral component of enamel. In a cosmetic oral care context, it is used to support smoother surfaces, reduce roughness, and help create the appearance of stronger, healthier teeth over time.

  • Targets micro-irregularities on enamel surfaces
  • Supports a smoother, more polished feel after brushing
  • Works as a gentler alternative to bleach-led brightening
